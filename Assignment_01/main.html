Some interesting facts about my Hometown

Hyderabad, known as the "City of Pearls," is rich in history and culture. Notable facts include:

  1. *Home to the World's Largest Film Studio*: Ramoji Film City holds this record
  2. *Famous for Biryani*: Hyderabad's culinary scene is renowned, especially for its biryani.
  3. *Biggest Monolithic Buddha Statue*: The city houses an impressive Buddha statue carved from a single rock.
  4. *Historic Significance*: It's one of India's oldest royal cities and was once the real home of the famous Koh-i-Noor diamond.
